# 👥 KeenKeeper
> **Keep Your Friendships Alive.** A clean, intuitive web application designed to help you track, manage, and nurture your personal relationships when life gets busy.

---

## 📌 Overview
Maintaining friendships can be difficult. **KeenKeeper** solves this by tracking your last contact with friends and reminding you to stay in touch. Whether it's a quick text, a long phone call, or a weekend video chat, KeenKeeper helps you visualize your communication habits and ensures no important relationship falls through the cracks.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### ⚡ Quick Check-In System
Log your interactions frictionlessly. Each action automatically updates your timeline and triggers a confirmation toast notification.
* 📞 **Call**
* 💬 **Text**
* 🎥 **Video**

### 📜 Interactive Timeline
* **Centralized History:** View all past interactions in a unified chronological feed.
* **Detailed Entries:** See the exact date, type of interaction, and context at a glance.
* **Smart Filtering:** Quickly sort your history by interaction type to find specific catch-ups.

### 📊 Friendship Analytics
* **Visual Insights:** Dynamic pie chart visualization of your communication patterns.
* **Distribution Tracking:** Easily see your preferred methods of contact (Calls vs. Texts vs. Videos) over time.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

Built with a modern, fast, and scalable frontend ecosystem:

* **Core:** `React.js`
* **Routing:** `React Router DOM`
* **Styling & UI:** `Tailwind CSS` | `DaisyUI` | `React Icons`
* **Data Visualization:** `Recharts`
* **Utilities:** `React Toastify`
* **Hosting:** `Vercel`
